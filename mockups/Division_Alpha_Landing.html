The Rhythm

Structure that creates discipline.

Every week follows the same Monday / Wednesday / Friday cadence. Consistency is the mechanism. AI drives the rhythm. Humans drive the accountability.

Monday
Declare
Tell your squad exactly what you'll deliver this week. Be specific. Be measurable. No "work on my business." Your squad will ask you what happened on Friday.
Wednesday
Signal
Green, yellow, or red on each goal. Two minutes. If you're stuck, your squad might have the answer before Friday. Mid-week check-ins increase completion rates by 45%.
Friday
Reflect
What landed. What didn't. What you learned. Celebrate wins. Be honest about misses. Your squad has your back — not your ego.
Week 3
The Dip
Motivation drops at the midpoint. We design for it. Goal adjustment amnesty. Squad gratitude. Reconnection to why you started. This is where operators separate from wishful thinkers.
Week 6
Completion
Final ceremony. Sprint report card. Gratitude round. Then you choose: continue with the same squad, reshuffle into a new one, or pause. Your call.
The Intelligence

Five AI agents running behind the scenes.

You interact with humans. AI handles the orchestration, pattern recognition, and personalisation that makes the whole thing work.

Matchmaker
Squad formation
Builds squads based on timezone, goal type, personality, and communication style. Not random — engineered.
Facilitator
Weekly rhythm
Drives the Mon/Wed/Fri cadence. Posts prompts, summarises check-ins, connects members who can help each other.
Coach
Private 1-on-1
Your personal coach. Refines goals, spots patterns, asks the questions you're avoiding. Available 24/7.
Guardian
Engagement health
Monitors squad activity. Catches disengagement before it becomes a decision to quit. Intervenes privately and gently.
Analytics
Operator score
Tracks goal completion, attendance, contribution, leadership, growth, and communication. Your score qualifies you for Tier 3.

Questions

Common questions.

Life happens. If you need to miss, communicate with your squad. The Guardian AI will check in privately if you go quiet. What's not okay is disappearing without a word — your squad is counting on you.
The Matchmaker AI considers timezone overlap (hard requirement), goal diversity, personality balance, experience level, and communication style. You'll be placed with people who complement your working style, not mirror it.
Monday declaration: 5 minutes. Wednesday check-in: 2 minutes. Friday reflection: 15-45 minutes (depending on whether you join the sync call or go async). The total weekly commitment is about 30 minutes. The rest is doing the actual work on your goals.
Your Coach refines your goals before the sprint, spots patterns in your behaviour across weeks (like consistently avoiding certain types of goals), asks questions you're not asking yourself, and provides private insight after each check-in. It's available 24/7 for goal discussion.
Division Alpha is a product of Oumafy. Islamic values of intention (niyyah), effort (amal), and trust (tawakkul) inform the design, but the platform is for anyone serious about accountability. You don't need to be Muslim to join.
At the completion ceremony, your squad votes: continue together, reshuffle into new squads, or pause. If 75%+ vote to continue, your squad stays intact. Your Operator Score, sprint history, and AI Coach context carry over across sprints.
Your Operator Score qualifies you. It tracks goal completion (25%), attendance (20%), squad contribution (20%), leadership (15%), growth (10%), and communication (10%). Top-performing operators receive an invitation at sprint completion. You can't buy your way in.
