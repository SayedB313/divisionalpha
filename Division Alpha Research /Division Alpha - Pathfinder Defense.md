- 1 human facilitator managing 20-30 AI-augmented groups instead of 3-5
- Revenue per facilitator: $20-30 people × $49 = $980-1,470/mo
- Still not viable unless human is founder (takes $0 salary initially) OR you layer multiple revenue streams

**The key shift:** [Research shows hybrid models (AI + human) outperform AI-only or human-only](https://careertrainer.ai/en/reports/ai-coaching-statistics/). This is new data since 2020 accountability failures.
