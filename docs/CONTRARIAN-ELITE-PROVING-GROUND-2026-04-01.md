### LENS 1 — THE EXECUTIONER
*The Single Fatal Flaw*

The fatal flaw hasn't changed from v1. It's just wearing a different costume.

You are still betting that an AI can create sustained engagement without human social stakes. In v1, the bet was "AI + pods." In v3, the bet is "AI Boss alone at $19."

The evidence hasn't changed:
- Real-world AI app retention: 3.9% at 15 days
- Prior research: "AI interactions feel repetitive, driving disconnection"
- Prior research: "Pure AI coaching fails because users can ghost a chatbot without social cost"

You removed pods (solving structural problems). But pods were also the only social consequence layer. Without them at ENTER, you have an AI chatbot with a sprint timer. The world has thousands of those.
